Twitter Integration Help

This guide will help you set up Twitter integration for automated social media posts from your online store.

Step 1: Create Twitter Developer Account

  • Go to Twitter Developer Portal
  • Sign in with your Twitter account
  • Click "Create Project" or "Create App"
  • Fill in your project details:
    • Project Name: Your Store Name + "eCommerce"
    • Use Case: "Building tools to engage with my audience"
    • Description: "Automated social media posts for my online store"

Step 2: Get API Credentials

  • In your Twitter Developer Dashboard, go to "Projects & Apps"
  • Select your app
  • Go to "Keys and Tokens" tab
  • Copy these credentials:
    • API Key (Consumer Key)
    • API Key Secret (Consumer Secret)
    • Access Token (if not generated, click "Generate")
    • Access Token Secret

Step 3: Configure Your Store

  • Contact the platform administrator with your Twitter credentials
  • They will configure the integration for your store
  • Once configured, your store will automatically post to Twitter when you create new stores or products

Note: Twitter integration allows your store to automatically share new store openings and product additions on Twitter, helping to increase visibility and engagement.
